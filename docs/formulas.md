# Formulas

## ROI

- ROAS = total revenue / total ad spend
- ACOS = total ad spend / total revenue
- Gross profit = total revenue - COGS
- Net profit = gross profit - shipping - platform fees - ad spend
- Net margin = net profit / total revenue
- Break-even ACOS = contribution profit before ads / total revenue

## Profit

- Gross profit = revenue - COGS
- Contribution profit = gross profit - shipping - platform fees
- Net profit = contribution profit - ad spend
- Net margin = net profit / revenue

## Inventory

- Days left = current inventory / average daily sales
- Reorder point = lead time days * average daily sales + safety stock

Status:

- Reorder Now if current inventory <= reorder point
- Watch if days left <= 30
- Healthy otherwise

## Product Research Scorecard

- Demand score: 25%
- Margin score: 25%
- Competition score: 20%
- Pain point score: 15%
- Differentiation score: 15%

Rating:

- Strong if weighted score >= 8
- Good if weighted score >= 6
- Weak if weighted score < 6
